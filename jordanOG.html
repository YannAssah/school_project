*Shoutout to the "Human Highlight Film"*

This is the Air Jordan I High OG "Atlanta Hawks" aka "Human Highlight Film" in the black, gym red and university gold color combination matching the Atlanta Hawks uniforms. This pair commemorates the 1988 Atlanta Hawks as one Jordan's "formidable foes." It features a mostly black and gym red upper with a white midsole and university gold accents on the Nike swoosh, the inner lining and the outsole. The black toe box and swoosh background panel features an all over glossy film reel print that represents Dominique Wilkins' nickname "The Human Highlight Reel". The insole features an emblem that says "1988-1996 Formidable Foes Honorary League" and "Challengers Of The Greatest Of All Time". In the late 1980's the Atlanta Hawks led by Dominique Wilkins, a rival of Mj, were one of the Chicago Bulls' toughest opponents. In the 1987-1988 season the Bulls and Hawks were tied for second place in their division but losing to the first seeded Boston Celtics. Earlier this year we saw two Air Jordan I OG releases that commemorated Larry Bird's Celtics and Patrick Ewing's Knicks. The AJ1 Retro "OG" series features the Nike Air logo on the tongue and outsole, like the original 1985 original release. Be sure to add this pair to your Air Jordan 1 collection.
